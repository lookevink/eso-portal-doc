# Overview

The Easy Street Offers CRM is a custom lightweight customer relationship management platform that supports your sales activities.

## Main Screens

This CRM currently contains five primary screens:

- **Home**: Home screen displays the CRM user manual. Once you have enough activity, this screen will be replaced with a dashboard showing your summaries.
- **My Leads**: My leads screen displays your assigned contacts and provides you a way to search through, organize, and prioritize your leads.
- **To-dos**: To-dos screen displays your scheduled tasks and provides you a way to search manage your tasks.
- **Appointments**: Appointments screen displays your scheduled appointments and provides you a way to manage your appointments.
- **Profile**: Profile screen allows you to change your password.

You can navigate between these screens using the tabs at the top right of the screen.

## Requesting New Accounts

We manually approve new users to this CRM. To request a new account, Please provide:

- First Name
- Last Name
- Email Address
- Desired Password (You may change this after your first login).
- Group/Firm Representing

If have a large number of accounts to set up, please provide the information above in a .CSV file to [ricky@easystreetoffers.com](mailto:ricky@easystreetoffers.com)
